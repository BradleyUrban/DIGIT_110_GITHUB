Corpus Historic 2009 Text

  • Across both Voyant Tools and AntConc, the data reveals a narrative centered heavily around characters such as Sara, Porter, and members of the Woodwards family, with strong thematic emphasis on a wedding.
  • Voyant’s word cloud and frequency trends highlight these recurring names and themes, while its summary statistics show a moderately varied vocabulary and conversational readability.
  • AntConc’s N-gram table confirms this focus by listing the most frequent two-word sequences, where combinations like “mr porter,” “a wedding,” “the family,” and “by woodwards” occur multiple times. Together, the tools indicate that the text is a character-driven story with repeated references to family relationships, wedding preparations, and interpersonal interactions, showing consistent thematic concentration throughout the document.
